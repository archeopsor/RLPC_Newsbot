**__Other Commands__**
*Parameters must be in order. Those in italics are optional, those underlined are case-sensitive.*



**{prefix}ping**: Shows the bot's current ping.

**{prefix}alerts**: Subscribes the current channel to Upset Alerts. Only usable if you have Manage_Channel perms in the server.

**{prefix}schedule** [team]: Shows the season schedule for the specified team.

**{prefix}roster** [team]: Gives basic information about a team.

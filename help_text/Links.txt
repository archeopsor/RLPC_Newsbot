**Link Commands**
*Shows links for important RLPC and RLPC News sites*

 - {prefix}rlpc shows RLPC invite link
 - {prefix}rlpcnews shows RLPC News invite link
 - {prefix}reddit shows the link to the RLPC subreddit
 - {prefix}apply shows the link to apply to join the RLPC News team
 - {prefix}invite shows the link to invite the RLPC Newsbot to your server
